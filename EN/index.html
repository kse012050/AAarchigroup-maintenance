Create the Great
New start 2023

AA Archigroup is an innovative global design company that leads the market with more competitive
design based on the trust built with technological knowledge.

For the past fifty years, AA Archigroup has dedicated itself to the advancement of architectural
design by merging creative ingenuity with accumulated technological expertise, thus maintaining
its position as a leading force in the high-tech design realm within Korea.

Presently, AA Archigroup is capitalizing on business synergies through the utilization of
AA Archigroup's 'AA Chain,' which encompasses 'Design-CM/Supervision-Developer,' consequently
establishing a groundbreaking paradigm for the real estate and construction industry.

Looking ahead, AA Archigroup envisions propelling itself forward as a Total Solution
Company by embracing challenges and fostering innovations.
  • Architecture

    We strive to achieve the highest quality designs that showcase distinctive
    design and technical expertise by fostering tight collaboration between specialized
    design teams in each field and our in-house specialized construction departments.

  • CM

    Leveraging our specialized knowledge and diverse project experience, we provide optimal
    construction management services that seamlessly integrate design, construction,
    and engineering technologies at every stage of the construction process.

  • DM

    We ensure prosperous business outcomes through comprehensive design management
    of intricate and diverse projects, including Design & Build and Turn-Key, offering
    unparalleled services such as value engineering and expedited construction timelines.
